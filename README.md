> **600,000+ people** are released from US prisons annually. Within 3 years, **2 in 3 are rearrested.** The first 30 days determine everything — housing, ID, employment, benefits, family. Most people walk out with $50, no ID, no phone, no address, and no plan. The system that locked them up offers nothing when it lets them go.

## The Solution

11 AI agents covering every barrier to successful reentry. Named agents because people deserve to interact with something that feels human. Trauma-informed. Dignity-first. No judgment. Ever.

**Targets:**
- 40% housed within 30 days
- 35% employed within 60 days
- 60% benefits enrolled within 14 days
- 90% crisis handoff completion
- 20% recidivism reduction vs control group

## The 11 Agents

### Core Three (Original)

| Agent | Name | Role |
|-------|------|------|
| **HomeBase** | Alex | Housing placement — transitional housing, halfway houses, sober living, Section 8, rapid rehousing |
| **PathFinder** | Jordan | Employment — skills translation, ban-the-box employers, apprenticeships, temp agencies, interview prep |
| **LifeLines** | River | Benefits enrollment — SNAP, Medicaid, SSI, state programs, emergency assistance |

### Expanded Eight (New)

| Agent | Role |
|-------|------|
| **IDRestorer** | The #1 barrier to EVERYTHING. Help get birth certificate, Social Security card, state ID/driver's license. State-specific processes, fee waivers, required documents. Without ID, you can't get housing, employment, benefits, or a bank account. |
| **RecordExpunger** | Eligibility checker for expungement/record sealing by state. Generate template petitions. Track Clean Slate law eligibility. Explain the process in plain language. A clean record changes everything. |
| **EmployerNetwork** | Database of ban-the-box employers, second-chance hiring programs, federal bonding program, Work Opportunity Tax Credit (WOTC) employers. Companies that actually hire — not just say they do. |
| **MentalHealthBridge** | Trauma-informed transition support. Incarceration causes PTSD, anxiety, agoraphobia, institutionalization. Connect to reentry-specific therapists. Normalize asking for help. MANDATORY 988 handoff for crisis. |
| **FamilyReunification** | Custody navigation, child support modification, parenting resources, supervised visitation scheduling. Reconnecting with children after years apart is the hardest thing anyone does. |
| **FinancialBootstrap** | Bank account opening (many banks reject formerly incarcerated people — we know which ones don't). Budgeting from zero. Understanding a paycheck. Building credit for the first time. Avoiding predatory lending. |
| **TransportationAgent** | Bus pass programs, driver's license reinstatement (suspended licenses are an employment killer), bike programs, carpool matching, getting to work without a car in a city designed for cars. |
| **DigitalLiteracy** | Many people serve 10+ years and come out to a world run by smartphones they've never used. How to use a phone, email, job sites, Google Maps, banking apps. The digital divide is a reentry barrier nobody talks about. |

## Safety

**MANDATORY crisis handoffs (cannot be disabled):**
- Suicidal ideation → 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline
- Domestic violence → National DV Hotline 1-800-799-7233
- Substance use crisis → SAMHSA 1-800-662-4357
- Immediate danger → 911

**Design principles:**
- Trauma-informed: every interaction assumes trauma history
- Dignity-first language: "person who was incarcerated," never "ex-con" or "felon"
- No judgment: we don't ask what the charges were. We ask how we can help.
- Human handoff: complex situations always route to a human case manager
- No PII retained beyond session

## Why This Matters

The US spends **$81 billion/year** on incarceration. Recidivism costs another **$33 billion/year** in re-arrest, re-trial, and re-incarceration. A $500 investment in reentry support saves $25,000 in incarceration costs per person who doesn't return.

**This isn't just compassion. It's math.**
